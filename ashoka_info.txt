# Ashoka Institute of Technology and Management, Varanasi

## Overview
Ashoka Institute of Technology and Management (AITM) is a premier engineering college in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, established in 2010. Affiliated with AKTU (Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University) and approved by AICTE. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, management, and technology.

## Courses Offered
- B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (120 seats)
- B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering (60 seats)
- B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (60 seats)
- B.Tech in Civil Engineering (60 seats)
- M.Tech in Computer Science (18 seats)
- MBA in Finance/Marketing/HR (60 seats)

Eligibility: For B.Tech - 10+2 with PCM (45% aggregate); JEE Main rank required.

## Admission Process
Admissions via UPTU/AKTU counseling based on JEE Main/UPSEE scores. 
Steps:
1. Register on AKTU website (aktu.ac.in).
2. Fill choices including Ashoka Institute.
3. Seat allotment via counseling rounds.
4. Document verification and fee payment (B.Tech fee: ~₹1.1 Lakh/year).
Direct admissions for management quota (20% seats).

Application deadline: Typically June-July for main round.

## Campus Facilities
- 20-acre campus with modern labs, library (50,000+ books), hostels (separate for boys/girls, ₹60,000/year).
- Sports complex, cafeteria, Wi-Fi, medical center.
- Placement cell: 80% placement rate; top recruiters - TCS, Infosys, Wipro (avg. package ₹4-6 LPA).
